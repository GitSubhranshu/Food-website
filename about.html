What is the secret receipe of our burgers

The Juicy Lucy is a burger with a molten cheese center. It is made by stuffing a cheese blend into the center of a ground beef patty, then grilling or pan-frying the patty until the cheese is melted and gooey.

The Smash Burger is a thin, crispy burger that is cooked over high heat. It is made by smashing a ball of ground beef onto a hot grill or griddle, then cooking it for a short period of time until it is browned and crispy.

The Blue Cheese Burger is a burger with a blue cheese crumble topping. It is made by topping a cooked burger patty with blue cheese crumbles, then grilling or pan-frying the patty for a short period of time until the blue cheese is melted and gooey.
