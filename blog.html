how to make burgers by admin / 20 june, 2023

For a more flavorful burger, use a blend of ground beef, such as 80/20 or 85/15. If you want a juicier burger, add a small amount of olive oil or vegetable oil to the ground beef mixture. Don't overwork the ground beef mixture when you're forming the patties. This will make the burgers tough. If you're grilling the burgers, make sure to preheat your grill to medium heat. This will help prevent the burgers from sticking to the grill. If you're pan-frying the burgers, use a non-stick pan and add a small amount of oil to the pan. This will help prevent the burgers from sticking to the pan. Don't overcook the burgers. They should be cooked to an internal temperature of 160 degrees Fahrenheit. Serve the burgers immediately with your favorite toppings.

how to make burgers by admin / 14 june, 2023

If you have time, let the ground beef mixture rest in the refrigerator for 30 minutes before forming the patties. This will help the burgers hold their shape better. If you're grilling the burgers, use a meat thermometer to check for doneness. The burgers should be cooked to an internal temperature of 160 degrees Fahrenheit. If you're pan-frying the burgers, don't press down on them while they're cooking. This will squeeze out the juices and make the burgers dry.
